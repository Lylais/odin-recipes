Joojeh Kabob

Joojeh Kabob is a popular Iranian dish consisting of marinated chicken skewers grilled to perfection. The marinade typically includes yogurt, lemon juice, saffron, and a mix of spices, which tenderize the chicken and infuse it with vibrant flavors. The grilling process enhances the smoky aroma and golden color, making Joojeh Kabob a favorite at gatherings and barbecues.

This dish is traditionally served with saffron-infused basmati rice and grilled vegetables, often accompanied by fresh herbs and flatbread. Joojeh Kabob represents the convivial spirit of Persian dining, where food is not only nourishment but also a way to bring people together. Its simplicity, combined with its bold flavors, makes it a staple in Iranian households and an essential dish for festive occasions.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomato purée
  • Greek yogurt
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Red kidney beans
  • Turmeric
  • Saffron
  • Lemon
  • Salt and pepper
  • Yellow onion and garlic
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Bloom the saffron
  2. Make the marinade
  3. Add the chicken
  4. Leave to marinate
  5. Skewer the chicken
  6. Grill
  7. Serve
