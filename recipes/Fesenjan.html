Fesenjan

Fesenjan is a traditional Persian stew known for its unique blend of sweet and savory flavors. It is made with ground walnuts and pomegranate molasses, which create a rich and tangy sauce. The dish typically features tender pieces of chicken or duck, though some variations include meatballs. The combination of the earthy walnuts and the tart sweetness of pomegranate molasses gives Fesenjan its signature depth of flavor, making it a beloved dish in Iranian cuisine.

Fesenjan is usually served with basmati rice, allowing the rich sauce to be absorbed into the grains. It is often prepared for special occasions and celebrations, as it symbolizes warmth and abundance. This dish highlights the sophistication of Persian cooking, where ingredients are carefully balanced to create a harmonious and unforgettable culinary experience.

Ingredients

  • Walnuts
  • Onion
  • Chicken Thighs
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Turmeric, Cinnamon and Saffron
  • Water
  • Pomegranate Molasses
  • Sugar
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Dried limes
  • Pomegranate Seeds

Steps

  1. Grind the Walnuts
  2. Toast the Walnuts
  3. Cook Onions
  4. Cook Chicken Thighs
  5. Add the Ground Walnuts
  6. Add Pomegranate Molasses, Sugar and Saffron
  7. Add the Chicken, Simmer and Season
